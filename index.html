This is my new note

Childhood Relationships May
Date: may 19, 2018

Ever since I was a pre-adolescent I have been fascinated by the theoretical limits of relationships. What starts out as hope soon becomes corrupted into a carnival of lust, leaving only a sense of chaos and the dawn of a new order. As subtle replicas become frozen through emergent and repetitive practice, the viewer is left with a testament to the limits of our culture.
